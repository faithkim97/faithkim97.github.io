Hi! My name is Faith Kim, and I'm a third year student at Smith College studying Computer Science with a focus in game development.

I am a game programmer for GlowLime Games, an organization located in Hampshire College dedicated to releasing cool games developed by Five college students. I primarily use Unity (C#) for the development process, but I also like to create small visual novels in Renpy for personal pleasures.

I'm always striving to write clean and efficient code for my projects, and I am ready to learn new skills to successfully develop a game.

I also love being involved in my house and Computer Science communities. I am the current Student Academic Advisor for my house, where I advise new students what courses to take, help them adjust to the new college environment, and provide information on registering for classes for both our home institution and Five Colleges.


I am also the Event Chair of Smithies in Computer Science, a student organization dedicated to inspiring more students to be involved in tech. We are currently organizing a tech lunch series learning more about the application process for tech internships, and we also plan on holding workshops for beginners on how to code a website in HTML/CSS; and our largest event will be Smith's Hackathon, which will be happening in Spring 2018.

On the fun side, I really enjoy reading tarot cards and astrologies for my friends!
