About Me

Hey everyone! Thanks for checking out my website! I'm Faith, and I'm a third year student at Smith College studying Computer Science.


I'm really interested in Computer Graphics and game programming, so I'm focusing my studies on making personal games using game engines like Unity and Unreal Engine, and I also love creating visual novels with RenPy! To take my game programming to the next level, I will be more active in GlowLime Games, a student organization in Hampshire College dedicated to creating video games.


In a team environment, I'm usually the one organizing the division of labor and directing people to specific duties. I really like to keep my team organized and efficient, but most importantly make the job as fun as possible for any projects we work on!

Outside of programming, I love playing music (I play the guitar, clarinet, and piano), drawing (I like to draw my visual novel characters on Paint Tool SAI), cooking, and writing!
